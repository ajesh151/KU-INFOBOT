eligibility|Applicants must have completed NEB (+2) Science or equivalent with a minimum aggregate GPA of 2.0 or 50% marks. They must have studied Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics.

pcb eligibility|Students from the Biology stream are eligible only if they have passed Additional Mathematics at the +2 level.

gce a level|Applicants must complete at least three A-Level courses and one AS-Level course including Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics with a minimum grade D in each subject.

ib diploma|Applicants must obtain at least 24 points in the IB Diploma and complete Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics with a minimum grade of 3 in each subject.

ctevt diploma|Applicants with a three-year CTEVT diploma in a related technical field with at least 50% aggregate marks are eligible to apply.

application|Applicants must complete the online registration process before the admission deadline.

online registration|Applications are submitted through the Kathmandu University online admission portal.

application process|The admission process includes online registration, entrance examination, publication of the merit list, program preference update and enrollment confirmation.

program change|Applicants can modify their program preferences within 48 hours after the merit list is published.

enrollment confirmation|Selected applicants must submit the Enrollment Confirmation Form to secure their admission.

architecture admission|Applicants for Bachelor of Architecture must qualify the KUCAT-CBT and pass an Aptitude Test that evaluates freehand sketching, spatial awareness and aesthetic sensitivity.

geomatics scholarship|Geomatics Engineering students may receive scholarships through collaboration with the Land Management Training Centre (LMTC).

tie breaking|If two applicants obtain the same entrance score, their +2 academic performance is used to determine the final ranking.
